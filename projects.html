Multimodal GenAI Compliance System

Katonic AI · Largest Governmental Real Estate Entity in Saudi Arabia · 2025
  • Problem: Manual review of architectural design images against internal policy standards — slow, inconsistent, and hard to scale.
  • Built: A multimodal GenAI pipeline where users upload design images and vision agents automatically check them against policy rules, generating a structured report across three categories — Compliant, Non-Compliant (with reasoning), and Needs Review.
  • Technical highlight: Integrated MinerU, a non-OpenAI-compatible model, by building a custom FastAPI wrapper deployed on the Katonic platform. Full agent architecture built on Katonic AI using Groq and custom-deployed models.
  • My role: End-to-end ownership — architecture, development, customer stakeholder alignment, and internal product coordination.
